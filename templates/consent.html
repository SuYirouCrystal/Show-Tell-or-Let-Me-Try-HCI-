Please review this information before starting the study. You may stop at any time by closing the browser.

Purpose

This study examines how different AI explanation formats affect decision-making, understanding, trust, and cognitive effort during product selection tasks.

What you will do

  • Complete 6 product-selection tasks in a web interface.
  • See AI recommendations shown in one explanation format for the full session.
  • Answer short confidence and AI-judgment questions after each task.
  • Complete a final questionnaire about your experience.

Time and data

The session should take about 8 to 12 minutes. The system records your choices, timing, condition, survey responses, and browser user-agent string. Do not enter sensitive personal information.

Voluntary participation

Participation is voluntary. You may stop at any time without penalty. If you do not want to participate, choose "I do not agree" below.

Risks

This is a minimal-risk study involving ordinary computer use. Some participants may experience mild fatigue or frustration while completing the tasks.
